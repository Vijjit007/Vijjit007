Eiffel tower

Paris, France

the Eiffel Tower is an iconic wrought-iron lattice tower and one of the most recognized structures globally. It offers breathtaking views of the city and is a symbol of both Paris and French engineering prowess.


Taj Mahal

Agra, India

The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum located in Agra, India. Built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, it is considered a masterpiece of Mughal architecture. The Taj Mahal is renowned for its stunning white marble exterior, intricate inlay work, and beautiful gardens.


Statue Of Liberty

New York, USA

Standing tall on Liberty Island in New York Harbor, the Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture. It was a gift from France to the United States, representing freedom and democracy. The statue has become an enduring symbol of American values and welcomes visitors to New York City.


Pyramids Of Giza

Cairo, Egypt

The Pyramids of Giza are ancient structures located on the outskirts of Cairo, Egypt. These monumental tombs, including the Great Pyramid of Khufu,are remnants of the Egyptian civilization and are considered one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.
